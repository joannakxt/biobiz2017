HISTORY

Since its inception in the year 2006, BioBiz has become an established, annual biomedical conference well-received and recognised by an increasing number of students and stakeholders of the biomedical industry. This success can be attributed to the student centric nature of BioBiz. Organised by students from the National University of Singapore (NUS) in collaboration with students from the Nanyang Technological University (NTU), our programmes are customised to meet the needs of undergraduates and alumni.

BioBiz celebrated its 10th year anniversary last year and was a resounding success. With over 600 participants, BioBiz 2015 has expanded its reach beyond university undergraduates to polytechnic students, which is a corollary of our increased publicity. The number of polytechnic students had increased 5-fold, from 4% in 2014 to 21% of the total number of participants in 2015. BioBiz 2015 has also garnered much positive feedback, with more than 90% of the participants reflecting that they knew more about the biomedical industry through the panel discussions. On top of that commendable figure, close to 75% of the participants displayed greater interest in the biomedical field after attending the conference.

Through the years, more than 4500 participants have benefitted from the successful BioBiz conferences. BioBiz 2016 aims to reach out to 650 participants and is committed to continue this legacy for the future talents of the biomedical industry.

VENUE

Getting there



By Train

MAX Atria has a dedicated MRT station next to Hall 6. Trains arrive every 5 minutes from Changi International Airport and Tanah Merah MRT station while it takes about 25 minutes from the city. By 2017, the Expo MRT station will become an interchange for the new Downtown Line, which will provide additional transport modes from MAX Atria to CBD areas such as Clarke Quay, Marina Bay and River Valley.

By Car

Situated at the crossroads of 3 main expressways : East Coast Parkway, Pan Island Expressway and Tampines Expressway, MAX Atria is a mere 15-minute drive from the CBD and a 5-minute ride to Changi International Airport.

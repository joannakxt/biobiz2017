ABOUT US

VISION

"To give aspiring talents a better perspective on Singapore's biomedical and pharmaceutical industry and at the same time empower the growth of these industries with promising talents."

MISSION

"To inspire and connect aspiring talents with experts in the biomedical field."

BioBiz is a biomedical conference that is organized for students by students. It aims to provide an interactive platform to help aspiring graduates and undergraduates gain insights and expose them to the myriad of opportunities available in the biomedical industry.

BioBiz 2017 will be the biggest BioBiz till date. We are targeting at least 800 participants this year. We also aim to include more polytechnic and ITE students who also have interest in the biomedical field to make up this number. BioBiz is committed to continue the legacy for the future talents of the biomedical industry and work towards the Singapore Skills Future Mission 2020.

CHAIRPERSON'S MESSAGE

With a successful decade long track record of connecting the leaders of future with the leaders of today in biomedical industry, I am delighted to present to you - BioBiz 2017. Every year in Singapore, BioBiz provides a platform for both the biomedical industry and the young talents from universities to find the right employee and the right employer, respectively. By bringing the key players in the biomedical industry and budding talents together, BioBiz has become the bridge that makes "supply meet demand" in Singapore's biomedical industry.

With growing participation from previous years, BioBiz 2017 is going to be even more exciting and fruitful for both the talents and talent hunters in biomedical arena. Building on previous years' success, we aim to expand our platform from a specialized niche in biomedical sciences to a wider one, incorporating biotechnology and medical technology industries. Along with the enthusiasm we have witnessed from undergraduates over the years, we foresee a higher participation by university postgraduates, polytechnic and ITE graduates, as we align ourselves with Skills Future Mission of Singapore, by holding parallel sessions for polytechnic and ITE audience and by expanding ourselves into R&D, healthcare and medical technology areas. This will provide specialists as well as technicians to the industries as well as a chance to propel the mission of building a skilled workforce in Singapore.

At a time when Singapore's world-class biomedical industry is driven by interdisciplinary knowledge, it is imperative that multidisciplinary talents converge and connect. Innovation in research, development and manufacturing of biomedical products has been a result of the synergistic efforts from cross-field experts working together. We aim to bring this fact to the attention of prospective biomedical talents through lively discussion sessions by field experts and key stakeholders, and also by displaying the traditional and novel jobs available in biomedical industry. At BioBiz 2017, our primary focus would be to provide participating companies with easier access to a wider pool of talents with varied backgrounds for the industries, while at the same time inciting a greater understanding of the existing career opportunities among the talents.

I believe, by bringing Singapore's best talents and top industrial players together, BioBiz 2017 presents the best platform for all involved - for companies to showcase their opportunities and for talents to unveil their potential, accelerating Singapore's vision for a strong workforce to push the horizon of biomedical industries in Singapore.

PANEL A

Global to Local: How Biomedical Industry is Changing our Society Today

Duration: 1.5 hours

Objective:
Discussion of the current trends and developments in the healthcare sector will give students more insights about the current problems and solutions. Have a global to local outlook to put this industry into context, from world to Asia to Singapore.

Possible subtopics to be discussed:
1. Current disease trends and solutions
2. New developments in biotech and medtech
3. How these 2 complement each other
4. Global trends to be addressed and narrows down to Singapore
    a. What are the drivers for growth of the industry in Asia?
    b. What would be Singapore's role in this growth in Asia?
    c. Career opportunities
5. Future of new medicines
6. Government companies and their future plans and investments

PANEL B

The Big Picture: Understanding Beyond the Pill

Duration: 1 hour

Objective:
For students to understand more about the biotech industry. Also discuss about the current market & employment trends as well as give insight to the different departments in a biotech company. Skills needed to break into the sector will be discussed as well as what each the sector has to offer for the students.

Possible subtopics to be discussed:
1. Future of the industry
2. Business perspectives and opportunities in Singapore and beyond
3. Local innovators
4. Specialisations and path that students can look into

PANEL C

Engineering New-grounds: Improving Healthcare through Medical Technologies

Duration: 1 hour

Objective: The Medtech industry is one that is up and coming. While there are large players with strong presence in the game, there are also many established start ups. As such, the panelist discussions will be mainly focused on the experiences and how working in a startup and large companies differ. The future of the industry is discussed, business opportunities and perspectives will be discussed as well.

Possible subtopics to be discussed:
1. Future of the industry
2. Business perspectives and opportunities in Singapore and beyond
3. Patents and innovations
4. Digital healthcare
5. Startups versus large international firms
6. Process of manufacturing to marketing - importance of manufacturing in this process
